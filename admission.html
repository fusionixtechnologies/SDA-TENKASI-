Required Documents

Keep the following documents ready during the admission process for a smooth and quick verification.

Birth Certificate

Valid proof of the student’s date of birth.

Transfer Certificate

Required for students joining from another school.

Passport Photos

Recent student photographs for records and documentation.

ID Proof

Parent or guardian identity proof for verification purposes.
